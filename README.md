# Red Rose by shineer: Premium Wellness & Affiliate Platform

This project powers the official online presence for "Red Rose by shineer," a premium wellness product, offering detailed information about its benefits, ingredients, and a straightforward purchasing process. It also serves as a comprehensive platform for individuals interested in joining the Red Rose affiliate network, outlining the opportunity, compensation structure, and enrollment paths.

## Features

- **Comprehensive Product Information**: Detailed sections on the Double Red Rose product, including its benefits, active ingredients, and recommended usage.
- **Seamless Affiliate Onboarding**: Dedicated pages explaining the affiliate opportunity, its advantages, placement options (Left Leg, Right Leg), and access to compensation guides.
- **Streamlined Purchase Process**: An intuitive flow for selecting product quantity, providing shipping details, viewing bank transfer information (Zelle), and submitting payment receipts via WhatsApp.
- **Direct WhatsApp Support**: Integrated contact and payment verification processes leveraging WhatsApp for efficient communication and customer service.
- **Engaging User Experience**: Dynamic and responsive interface built with modern web technologies, featuring smooth animations and clear navigation.
- **Customer Testimonials & Attachments**: Displays real customer feedback with ratings and includes an option to view supporting attachments.
- **Error Handling and Notifications**: Utilizes `sonner` for user-friendly toast notifications for form submissions and critical actions.

## Usage

This platform provides two primary user flows: purchasing the Double Red Rose product and joining the affiliate network.

### Purchasing the Product

1.  **Navigate to the Purchase Page**: Visit the "Purchase Product" link in the navigation or directly access `/purchase-product`.
2.  **Review Product Details**: On the purchase page, you will see the product name, unit price (with USD/NGN conversion toggles), and an input for your shipping information.
3.  **Enter Shipping Information**: Fill in your full name and delivery address in the provided fields.
4.  **Adjust Quantity**: Use the `+` and `-` buttons to set the desired quantity of the product. The total amount to pay will update automatically.
5.  **Make Payment**: Review the provided bank transfer details (Zelle email). Transfer the total amount to the specified account.
6.  **Upload Receipt**: Click the "Upload Receipt" button to select your payment receipt image or PDF file from your device. This selection is crucial for verification.
7.  **Submit & Continue on WhatsApp**: After filling in your details and selecting your receipt, click the "Submit & Continue on WhatsApp" button. This action will redirect you to WhatsApp with a pre-filled message containing your order summary.
8.  **Send Receipt on WhatsApp**: Attach the receipt file (the one you selected in the previous step) to the WhatsApp chat and send the message.
9.  **Monitor Payment Status**: You will be redirected to the "Payment Status" page, where your order status will be displayed as "Pending" until verification is complete. The system will guide you on further steps, including options to change your receipt or start a new order.

### Joining the Affiliate Network

1.  **Explore the Affiliate Page**: Navigate to the "Become an Affiliate" link in the navigation or directly access `/become-an-affiliate`.
2.  **Understand the Opportunity**: Read through the sections explaining what the opportunity entails and why it's easy to share.
3.  **Choose Your Placement**: The page presents options to join the "Left Leg" or "Right Leg." Click the respective "Join" button, which will direct you to the external enrollment portal for Affluence Global.
4.  **Review Compensation Guide**: You can also download the "Compensation Guide" (PPTX file) to understand the reward structure in detail before making a decision.
5.  **Contact for Support**: If you have further questions, use the "Contact" page to send a message via WhatsApp for assistance.
